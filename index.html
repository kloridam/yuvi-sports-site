About Yuvi

Built for competition, remembered for style.

Yuvi's story is built on balance. Soccer brings teamwork, intensity, and the thrill of reading the game in real time. Golf adds patience, discipline, and the ability to stay calm when every shot depends on focus.

Together, they show two sides of the same personality: someone who loves to compete, enjoys getting better, and keeps showing up with confidence whether the moment is loud or quiet.
